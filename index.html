Jatra Axom

Founded: 12 May 2025

Jatra Axom is a dedicated news and media platform committed to delivering timely and reliable updates to the people of Assam. With a mission to inform, alert, and empower citizens, we cover a wide range of topics — from local developments and cultural events to national affairs and emergency alerts.

Rooted in the values of integrity, accuracy, and public service, Jatra Axom aims to be the voice of Assam — bridging information gaps and ensuring that no update goes unnoticed. Our focus is simple: to keep you informed, aware, and ahead.
