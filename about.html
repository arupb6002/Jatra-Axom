About Jatra Axom

Founded on 12 May 2025 by Priyanshu Kalita

Our Mission

Jatra Axom is a dedicated news platform serving Assam with timely, transparent, and trustworthy information. In a world of misinformation, we stand as a reliable voice committed to keeping our community informed and aware.

Founded by Priyanshu Kalita, we focus on delivering news that matters - from local developments to cultural celebrations, weather updates to political shifts.

Our Values

Truth: We prioritize verified information over speed.

Community: We amplify voices from every corner of Assam.

Culture: We promote Assamese traditions responsibly.

Clarity: We cut through noise to deliver essential news.
