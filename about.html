Our Story

Jatra Axom is a dedicated news platform committed to serving Assam with accurate, timely information. In an era of misinformation, we stand as a beacon of reliability, keeping our community informed and empowered.

Founded by Priyanshu Kalita, we deliver comprehensive coverage - from grassroots developments to cultural events, weather alerts to political updates - always prioritizing what matters most to Assamese people.

Our Values

Truth First

We verify before we publish, ensuring every story meets our high standards of accuracy.

Community Focus

We amplify diverse voices across Assam, especially those often overlooked.

Cultural Pride

We celebrate and preserve Assam's rich heritage through responsible storytelling.
